Historic Citadel

The Traditional City:
The Casbah

Step into the winding cobblestone alleys of the legendary Casbah of Algiers, a historic citadel and UNESCO World Heritage site perfectly recreated in Roblox's voxel style.

Explore the stunning traditional architectural design, loaded with authentic white-washed plaster walls, traditional dark wooden mashrabiya balconies, and historic brick roofs. Pull up a classic white plastic chair under the stars to chat with friends, listen to the lofi synthesizer, and soak in the authentic community vibes beneath the glowing minaret.
